Let's begin

15 January 2019 Data Science, Business, Engineering
The idea of starting a blog to reflect on my experience as a data scientist was formed during useR! 2018 conference. Until then, I did not know that I belong to such a huge community of R users and developers who professionally and openly contribute to enhancing R features and functionalities as well as improving other users’ experience. I was especially impressed by Steph de Silva’s keynote talk. Given my extensive use of R and reliance on other users and professionals’ experience shared through blogs, online books, social media and StackExchange, I decided to contribute to this amazing community by sharing my personal experience with R, specifically in the context of business analytics and engineering, through a personal blog.
